minpower is an open source toolkit for students and researchers in power systems. It is designed to make working with ED, OPF, and UC problems simple and beautiful. The goal is to foster collaboration with other researchers and to make learning easier for students.

Solving problems is as easy as two lines of script:

from minpower import solve
solve.problem('mycommitment/')
