Why minpower?¶

minpower is designed to make solving ED, OPF, and UC problems easy and beautiful. Traditional approaches to solving these problems are limited by:

  • low level languages
  • expensive software
  • being solver dependent

Behold minpower!¶

  • written in Python
  • free and open source
  • can use many solvers
  • comparable solution times
